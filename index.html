"I've always admired graphic designs on different sorts of things and appreciated the work behind them, which led me to take the first step to study graphic design at Vancouver Career College while also working as a Product Evaluator at Oxygen8 Solutions. Other than graphic design, I enjoy listening to music, attending live music events, watching movies with friends, and going out for walks in the sun. Some of my favorite artists include Japanese Breakfast, Alvvays, and Snoh Aalegra.
